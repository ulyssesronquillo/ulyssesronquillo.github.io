Ordered List

Aug 18, 2006 • ulysses

I can’t recall having an ordered list entry. As a sample, I’m adding David Letterman’s recent top ten list. He does it a little differently in reverse order. I’m flipping it around for the reader’s sake.

Top Ten Signs Your Bought A Bad Computer

  1. When you tell it to print, it tells you to go screw yourself
  2. The mouse bit you
  3. It’s made by IBN
  4. You move the pointer around by licking the screen
  5. For better internet reception, salesman includes pair of rabbit ears
  6. Tech support number is a Silicon Valley Applebee’s
  7. Box reads “Pre-loaded with hundreds of viruses!”
  8. Runs on Windows ’78
  9. In the morning you have to defrost it
  10. Runs on 200 “D” batteries

As I suspected, my CSS ordered list was broken. It was showing squares instead of a numerical list. Well, a couple of tweaks in CSS did the trick. The numerical list is back! Woohoo! Small victories.

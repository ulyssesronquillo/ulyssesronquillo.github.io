my camera setup

May 25, 2025 • ulysses

Here’s my camera setup:

  1. Nikon D90
  • Nikon 18-200mm F3.5-5.6 G ED DX VR

APS-C lenses are not fully compatible with a full frame cameras and are typically not recommended. You can mount APS-C lenses in full frame cameras, but you will see heavy vignetting in the corners.

  1. Nikon D700
  • Nikon 24mm f2.8 AF-D FX
  • Nikon 35mm f2 AF-D FX
  • Nikon 50mm F1.8 AF-D FX
  • Nikon 50mm F1.4 AF-D FX
  • Nikon 24-85mm F3.5-4.5 G FX VR

You can use full frame lenses and in APS-C cameras, but it’s cheaper to buy APS-C lenses for APS-C cameras.

  1. Fujifilm XT30 ii
  • TTArtisan 27mm F2.8 AF
  • Fujifilm XC 15-45mm F3.5-5.6 OIS PZ

All Fujifilm cameras are APS-C, therefore all lenses are APS-C as well.

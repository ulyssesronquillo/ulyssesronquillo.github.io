ImageStation

Dec 3, 2005 • ulysses

I’ve selected a few prints to send to friends who prefer to see photographs the old fashion way (not online). I have a color printer capable of printing photos. I could try that, but I’m no too thrilled about the quality. Plus, the cost of buying more photo paper may not be warranted if I’m printing only a few pictures.

I have several options. Kinkos‘ has stations for printing, but the cost is $0.49 for a 4 x 6 print. A bit expensive. So, I decided to look elsewhere. I went to Shutterfly which I have used before many years ago. I also looked at KodakGallery and ImageStation.

The prices for printing 4 x 6 prints are affordable. Shutterfly charges $0.19, while KodakGallery charges for $0.15 and ImageStation at $0.12. I went with ImageStations since it was backed by Sony and it is also the cheapest. It wouldn’t hurt trying it out.

ImageStation gives you an option to pick up your photos at any Ritz Camera near you, in my case the San Francisco Bay Area. I opted for a home delivery since I didn’t want to drive 10 or so miles to the nearest Ritz Camera store.

Well, the jury is still out since I’m still waiting for the prints to arrive via standard mail. I’ll keep you updated later.

Top 40 Rich Filipinos

Jan 7, 2007 • ulysses

In a country where most of the 80 million inhabitants make less than $5000 per year, here’s a list of the Top 40 Richest Filipinos based on net worth according to Forbes Magazine. Nearly half of the list are Chinese-Filipinos. Even more remarkable, the list includes 4 women and even a distant relative. The figures below are in US dollars.

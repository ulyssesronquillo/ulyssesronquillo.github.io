High Speed Train

Apr 3, 2007 • ulysses

When I toured Europe several years ago, I caught a train between Sevilla and Madrid traveling at a speed of 180 miles per hour. It was fast. I remember looking out and seeing trees and posts blurring as the train bulleted its way through the Spanish countryside. France recently tested a train running at 357 miles per hour. That’s twice as fast. That’s amazing. We need them here in the USA. Imagine traveling from Los Angeles to San Francisco in just one hour.

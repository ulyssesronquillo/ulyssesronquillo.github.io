Google Filling Up the Page

Apr 14, 2006 • ulysses

First it was just a search engine, then the email, the desktop, the toolbar, the video, the maps, froogle, blogger, picassa and now the calendar. Talking about domination. Google is starting to fill up just about any space on the web which makes Yahoo and Microsoft very envious. If you look at Google’s stock, it has grown exponentially although it took quite a bit of a hit last quarter. Nonetheless, the meteoric rise of a company has been remarkable despite the internet bubble a few years ago. Google can be commended for innovation. They have continued to come up with cool stuff year after year. To see what Google currently offers, check out all the services offered here. One more thing before I sign off, Google calendar is the latest gizmo which I already mentioned above. If you like to view it, just visit calendar.google.com.

iPod Nano

Jan 6, 2006 • ulysses

How would you like to lose an iPod Nano inside a treadmill? I was at the gym tonight getting my 30 minutes of cardio work when a girl in in front of me drops her iPod Nano on the treadmill. The miniscule Nano found its way inside a small crack on the side of the conveyor belt.

A 24 hour fitness employee came to her aid and lifted the machine to its side. The Nano was nowhere to be found. It’s buried way inside the treadmill. One screwdriver, a prayer and two hours later, the Nano was rescued. The Nano didn’t seem to have any signs of trauma or carbon monoxide poisoning.

Needless to say, the owner was ecstatic to be reunited with the popular player. I like happy endings.
